Inside Our Nairobi Writing Camp

14 Feb 2025 · 3 min read

Earlier this year we gathered artists from Kenya, Nigeria and South Africa for a week‑long retreat in Nairobi. Set in a secluded studio complex, the camp gave producers and vocalists space to experiment freely.

Beats were swapped, styles were blended and by the final showcase we had a dozen genre‑bending demos. The collaborative energy sparked unexpected pairings and the seeds of several upcoming releases.

Beyond the music, the camp built lasting friendships across borders. Many of the writers are already planning follow‑up sessions and remote projects to keep the momentum alive.
