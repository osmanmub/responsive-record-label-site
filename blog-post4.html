Introducing Rydm and the Alafia Collective

15 December 2024 · 3 min read

We are thrilled to unveil Rydm, a new platform championing independent African artists. This launch article shares how the project grew into the Alafia Collective and what that means for creators.

Our mission is simple: provide artists with the resources, community and fair deals they need to thrive globally. From management to label services and publishing, we are building a collaborative home for the continent’s next icons.
