Introducing Rydm & Alafia Collective: My First Jam Session

15 December 2024 · 3 min read

Before there was a label or a roster, a few friends came together under the name Rydm. We squeezed into a tiny room with battered instruments and no real plan beyond making music.

That impromptu gathering became our very first jam session. We played late into the night and realised how powerful a collective spirit could be.

You can still hear that raw energy on our debut release. Listen on Spotify.
