What we do

Artist services, connected.

Alafia brings discovery, creative support, management, marketing, distribution and publishing into one connected team—built around the work an artist needs at each stage.

01

Management & Representation

We help shape the decisions around an artist’s career: priorities, partnerships, opportunities and the team around the work.

How we deliver

  • Career strategyGoals, release priorities and timing that support the bigger picture.
  • Partnership developmentBrands, collaborators and opportunities that fit the artist’s direction.
  • Team coordinationKeeping the key people and conversations aligned around each move.

02

Label & Artist Services

We support the journey from an early idea to a release that can find its audience—without treating every artist or project the same way.

How we deliver

  • Discovery & A&RIdentifying potential, repertoire and a creative direction worth building on.
  • Creative supportConnecting the right writers, producers and collaborators, then shaping the assets around the work.
  • Campaigns & distributionRelease planning, marketing narrative and delivery coordination for each project.

03

Publishing & Rights

We help creators make their music work as a long-term asset, with thoughtful attention to compositions, rights and commercial opportunity.

How we deliver

  • Composition strategySupporting the catalogue, collaborations and opportunities around the songs.
  • Rights & royalty oversightHelping coordinate rights management and royalty collection.
  • Sync & commercial opportunitiesPositioning compositions for relevant placements and partnerships.

How we work

The right support, at the right stage.

  1. ListenWe begin with the artist, the work and the opportunity in front of them.
  2. Build a strategyWe define a focused path instead of applying a fixed formula.
  3. Assemble the service mixManagement, creative, marketing, distribution and publishing come together where useful.
  4. Deliver and learnWe bring the plan to life, then use what each release teaches us to inform the next one.
